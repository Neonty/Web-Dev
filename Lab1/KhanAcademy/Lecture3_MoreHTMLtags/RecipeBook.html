Banana Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredient Amount
Banana 1 large
Egg 1

Mash the banana in a bowl until smooth. Add the egg and mix well.

Heat a pan and cook small pancakes until golden on both sides.

Greek Salad

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredient Amount
Tomatoes 3
Feta cheese 100 g

Chop all vegetables into medium-sized pieces.

Add feta cheese, olives, and olive oil, then gently mix.

Chicken Soup

  • Time: 45 minutes
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredient Amount
Chicken breast 300 g
Carrots 2

Boil the chicken for 20 minutes and remove the foam.

Add vegetables, cook until soft, and season to taste.
